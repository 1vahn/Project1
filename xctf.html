Cross Country and Track & Field

A common misconception is that cross country and track are similar sports. They are not. Let's break down the differences.

Cross Country

This is the sport you sign up for when you realize that your life calling is to wake up at 4 am every Saturday to go run in the woods. People can say that they like this sport, but usually they're lying.

Track & Field

While you're still running in track, the difference is that track is a little less miserable. Just a little less. And also you're running on a flat rubber oval instead of up a muddy 60 degree hill.
